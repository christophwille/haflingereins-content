All this information is in both the owners manual and the repair manual. If you are going to own a Haflinger, I would suggest you buy both. They can be both bought as reprints, so do not think you have to buy expensive "original" copies.

SAE 30 during winter (if your winters are mild) or SAE 40 for summer ~ Oil change approx 1.75lr.

Gearbox / differential / stroke = SAE 90 GL4 spec. 4lrs should allow you to buy Sufficient to change the oil in all places.

 

Even if you get some garage to do the work for you, BUY THE MANUALS - you can let the garage use them to do the work for you correctly.
