There should be rubber between the top of the tank and the bodywork and rubber on the straps between the tank and the straps at the bottom of the tank as it come round the sides of the tank so you do not have metal rubbing on metal - that eventually eventually rub a hole in you petrol tank causing a leak which could result in a fire.
